I no longer know who I am. I lost that along [the
Way](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=688YaaEePwE).

Sometimes I write songs. Even [somer](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Somerville,_Massachusetts) times I perform them.

I am often [dancing](https://contactimprovboston.com/).

I'm endlessly fascinated by the relationship between intuition and logic and how charts and diagrams
mediate that relationship.

Previous projects:

- [Bluefish](https://arxiv.org/pdf/2307.00146) — a web-based diagramming library ([bluefishjs.org](https://bluefishjs.org/))
- [Animated Vega-Lite](https://vis.csail.mit.edu/pubs/animated-vega-lite/) — unifying animation with an interactive grammar of graphics
- Mantis — a screen magnification tool
- Benthic — a screen reader tool for charts and diagrams
- [Semi-Formal Notebooks](https://vis.csail.mit.edu/pubs/semi-formal-design-space/) — a notebook for semi-formal programming
- Twofish — a direct manipulation editor for Bluefish
